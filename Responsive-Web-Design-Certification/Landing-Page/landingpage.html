Who We Are

Founded in 2023, Sarah's Cookies is a family-owned cookie bakery that serves the Tigard area. We are committed to quality ingredients and serving each batch of cookies with love. If you're looking for cookies like mom used to bake, then look no further than Sarah's Cookies.
